Our Values

timeless:

they last you a lifetime
Timeless pieces are characterized by their versatility, simple yet sophisticated designs, and quality craftsmanship

CHIC:

Whether it's a well-tailored blazer, a minimalist dress.. chic clothing transcends time, making a statement that is both timeless and contemporary.

CASUAL:

Key elements of casual fashion include comfortable fabrics, versatile silhouettes, and a mix of basic and statement pieces. It provides a canvas for self-expression, allowing individuals to effortlessly navigate their day-to-day activities with a sense of ease and authenticity.

Sustainability

MADAMEBELLE is a slow fashion brand, ethically made in Tunisia, and delivered to you in a recyclable plastic free packaging.

Materials and fabrics:

Our goal is to create long-lasting, high quality and timeless pieces.

  • Natural fibers, linen and cotton represent 90% of our fabric range.
  • Our fabric selection is based on quality, longevity and wearability.

Supply chain and manufacturing:

We are committed to a responsible and ethical production.

  • We work with a factory and artisans based in Tunisia and we have strict requirements on safety, human rights and fair wage.
  • Our supply chain is based in Tunisia and we source our materials locally.

limited waste:

  • Limited series to meet our demands and to avoid overproduction and its impact on the environment.
  • Upcycling approach: We use fabric dead-stocks and transform them into high quality pieces.
  • We make sure our fabric waste is minimal during the cutting and manufacturing process.

packaging:

Our packaging is eco-friendly: it is plastic free, reusable and recyclable.
